Anguished Girlfriend Collapsed When She Heard of Paul Walkers Death

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  172 words  |  Tandra Barner

The untimely death of actor Paul Walker has sent shock waves throughout Hollywood. While investigations are still being conducted about the circumstances around his death his family, loved ones, and adoring fans are mourning. Walker’s long-time girlfriend Jasmine Pilchard-Gosnell was so shocked when she heard the news she collapsed. According to RadarOnline, Pilchard-Gosnell fell to the ground when told about the death of her boyfriend.

Reports are still somewhat sketchy surrounding Walker’s accident and death. He was traveling home from a charity car show in Santa Clara, CA on Saturday when his car crashed. There are some reports indicating Walker was going over 100 MPH on a 45 MPH highway. Regardless of the situation, it’s a tragedy to lose someone that young.

Walker’s girlfriend is said to be in “so much anguish” she is being comforted by her mother and loved ones. Walker leaves behind his family, girlfriend, and 15-year old daughter, Meadow, who recently moved in with him full-time. Our prayers are with them all.
