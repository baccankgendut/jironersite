Blue Box Chapter 127:- Blue Box is a popular manga series written and illustrated by Kouji Miura. It follows the story of Taiki Inomata, a high school badminton player who has a crush on his childhood friend and basketball star, Chinatsu Kano. However, their relationship becomes complicated when Chinatsu moves into Taiki’s house due to her parents’ work. The manga is a romantic comedy that explores the themes of sports, friendship, and love.

The manga is serialized in Shueisha’s Weekly Shonen Jump magazine since April 12, 2021. As of November 15, 2021, it has 30 chapters collected in four tankobon volumes. The manga has received positive reviews from critics and fans, who praised its realistic and relatable characters, charming art style, and heartwarming story.

Recap of Previous Chapter 126

In the previous chapter of Blue Box, we saw Taiki and Chinatsu having a conversation on the phone.
