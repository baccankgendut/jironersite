Apart from a great musical work for the past five years, Kane Brown has a supportive family and a great wife. Moreover, within some months, his excitement is going to be double after welcoming his first child with his significant other.

American country music singer, Kane Brown is well-known from his 2015 song, Closer. His other hit song is 2017 release What Ifs that became successful in having the simultaneous number one spot on all five main Billboard country charts in October 2017.

Kane Brown Bio & Family

The singer was born as Kane Allen Brown on 21 October 1993 in Chattanooga, Tennessee. He belongs to a multi-racial family background where his mother, Tabatha Brown, is a Caucasian, and an African American father raised him. He went to Lakeview-Fort Oglethorpe High School in Fort Oglethorpe, Georgia, and the Red Bank, Ridgeland, and Soddy-Daisy High School.

Kane stands at the height of 6 feet 2 inches (1.87 m) tall and weighs around 83 kg.

Career & Net Worth

The 25-year-old has a net worth of $6 million from his musical career since 2014. He got massive fame and fortune releasing his 2015 hit track, Closer where the Kickstarter funded him. Some of his other commercially successful songs include Used to Love You Sober (2015) and Lose It (2018).

Apart from the hit songs, Kane makes a great fortune from tour events. In 2016, he got to open the Dig Your Roots Tour with American country music duo, Florida Georgia Line. Some of his upcoming tour events are Music City Center, Nashville from 6 June to 9 June 2019 which finally ends on 11 October 2019 at Global Life Park in Arlington.

Kane’s Wife About To Expect A Baby!

The Closer singer and his wife, Katelyn Jae are almost a quarter year celebrating their first marriage. They went to exchange wedding vows on 12 October 2018 at Mint Springs Farm in Nolensville, Tennessee, where only 200 close family and friends were the attendees. Just a year back, he proposed his wife on Easter Sunday in 2017.

Less than a couple of months of marriage, Kane announced them expecting their first child on 15 April 2019 via IG. The pair also did not hesitate to reveal expecting a baby girl during the Billboard Music Awards on 1 May 2019.
