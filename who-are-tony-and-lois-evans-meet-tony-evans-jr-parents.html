Tony Evans Jr’s Music Career

Epic Records Even More released his first album on June 8, 2004. The follow-up second studio album, Letting Go with Integrity Music, was released on March 7, 2006. The Bridge, his third studio album, would be released on the EMI Gospel label on June 10, 2008. Evans’ first-holiday album with INO, What Christmas Means, was released on October 2, 2009. Evans’ fourth studio album, Undisguised, was released on January 26, 2010, via INO Records, which eventually changed its name to Fair Trade Services. Evans made his fifth studio album, Home, accessible via Fair Trade Services on November 15, 2011.

Real Life/Real Worship with Fair Trade Services, Evans’ sixth studio album, was released on April 8, 2014, after the finale of The Voice. In terms of sales, it was his most successful album release. According to the Billboard Hot 200, the album debuted at number 60 on the Billboard charts for the week of April 26, 2014. The album debuted at number four on the Top Christian Albums list the following week. The album also placed second in sales on the Top Gospel Albums list.
