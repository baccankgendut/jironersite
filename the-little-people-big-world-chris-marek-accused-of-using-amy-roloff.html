The Little People, Big World (LBPW) spoilers suggest that Chris Marek is using Amy Roloff for fame. Some fans ripped into him after the latest episode of the TLC series. They have second thoughts about her marriage. Keep on reading to learn more.

LBPW Spoilers: Amy Roloff Marries Chris Marek

According to the Little People, Big World spoilers, Amy Roloff met her husband Chris Marek in 2016. The couple met at a mixer. Amy was still finalizing her divorce from Matt Roloff. However, she wanted to get back out into the dating world.

Amy and Matt Roloff were married for almost 30 years. The dating scene changed a lot since then. Amy wanted to see if she could find someone new at a mixer.

She ended up falling in love with Chris. They got married in August 2021. Their relationship works for them.

Chris even gets along with her ex-husband. He’s gotten scrutiny for that during Season 25 of Little People, Big World. There has been more focus on their blossoming bromance. Some LBPW fans wonder if the two men meet to talk about her behind her back.

Thankfully, Chris has fit in with the Roloff family. He even bonded with Zach and Tori Roloff’s children. He’s even appeared on episodes of Little People, Big World.

When Amy and Chris aren’t filming for the TLC series, they often travel and try out some of her dishes together.

From the surface, it seems like the perfect marriage. However, some LBPW fans get the creeps from Chris. They think he’s only using Amy for fame. Other fans claim he has a weird obsession with her health complications.

Little People, Big World Spoilers: Chris Marek Gets Scrutiny

Over on Reddit, LBPW fans expressed their uncertainty about Amy Roloff’s marriage. Most of them feel uneasy about Chris Marek. They noticed his reaction to Amy getting sick, including her other health problems involving her Achondroplasia.

“I’m getting extreme sketchy vibes from Chris. He’s smiling when Amy is talking about her possibly getting sick or having complications from having dwarfism, and says he wouldn’t worry about it until it’s a real problem,” the original poster wrote.

“Guy is a creep!!! Amy is stupid! I am an adult born w a neuro disability-It is a thing. People so seek out disabled people for either fetishes, or to convince themselves they’re a good person or to abuse [SIC],” one user responded.
