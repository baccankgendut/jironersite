Both Catherine Zeta-Jones And Michael Douglas Share Their Birthday, September 25

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Martina Birk

The singer of Chicago posted a photo to Instagram to show her fans how she and her hubby celebrated their birthdays together on Sunday. Their birthdays are one day apart.

Douglas turned 78 years old, while Zeta-Jones became 53 years old. So, we are celebrating our Birthday!! In one of her posts, the actress could not contain her excitement. Even though we have been sharing our special day for the past 24 years, I am still looking forward to it. I love you!!! A la votre!!!!!!!!!!

After some time had passed, Zeta-Jones uploaded a video to her social media account showing herself and Douglas traveling in a private jet to an unknown location.

The actress greeted viewers at the beginning of the about 15-second clip by exclaiming, “It’s our birthday!” before returning to Douglas and wishing him a happy birthday and saying, “Happy birthday, honey.”

The actor then chimed in, wishing the woman a happy birthday and calling her “darling.” He was sitting a few feet away and giving a thumbs up from his seat.

Douglas, for his part, acknowledged the event by publishing a congratulatory post on Instagram that featured a picture of the couple holding each other when they were near the Taj Mahal.

Best wishes for your Birthday, Catherine! He captioned the photo with the phrase, “I love you always and forever.”

In 1998, while attending a film festival together, the two Hollywood icons finally crossed paths. Later on, Douglas said that he had made things a little unpleasant at the moment in an earnest attempt to charm the woman who would later become his wife.

During a presentation on The Jonathan Ross Show in 2016, he recounted that after about a half hour of hanging out together for the first time, he told her, “You know, I’m going to be the father of your children.” He said this to her after telling her that he would be their children’s father.
