Early Life and Career Beginnings

Born Mathilde O’Callaghan Notaro in the heart of Mississippi, Tig Notaro embarked on a journey that would take her from the southern charm of her birthplace to the glittering stages of stand-up comedy across the country. Despite the challenges of school that led to her dropping out and obtaining a GED, Notaro’s spirit was undeterred.
