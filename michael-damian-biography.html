Who is Michael Damian?

Michael Damian is an American actor, producer, and recording artist. Michael Damian is best known for his role in the soap opera ‘The Young and the Restless as Danny Romalotti.

Age, Parents, Nationality, and Education

Michael Damian was born on April 26, 1962, in San Diego, California, USA. His birth name is Michael Damian Weir and he is currently 61 years old.

He is a huge fan of Julie London since a young age, and he watched her on ‘Emergency!’ in 1972. His best friend/acting mentor was Jeanne Cooper.

He has not mentioned his parents, siblings, and his childhood. He holds American citizenship but his ethnicity is unknown.

Michael Damian: Professional Life, Career

Michael Damian started his music career as a member of his family band, ‘The Weirz’ and he released his two self-titled albums in 1975 and 1979.

He then made his appearance on American Bandstand in a cover of the Eric Carmen tune ‘She Did It.

Michael was allowed to play the part of singer Danny Romalotti on the daytime television series ‘The Young and the Restless.

Michael Damian played in the series for 12 years and he played the starring role of Joseph in the Broadway production of Andrew Lloyd Webber and Tim Rice’s musical, ‘Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat’ in which the production set a single-day box-office record.

He along with the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat had 231 performances at the Minskoff Theatre from November 10, 1993, to May 29, 1994.

Michael has released five albums with eight top 40 hits which include his number one cover of the David Essex song ‘Rock On’, which was certified gold in 1989.

For his ‘Was It Nothing at All’, he won a BMI Song-Writing Award.

He debuted his film directional with an indie comedy ‘Hot Tamale’ which he wrote with his wife Janeen.

Some of the films he directed are ‘Moondance Alexander’, ‘Flicka 2’, ‘Marley & Me: The Puppy Years’, ‘Flicka: Country Pride’, and ‘Princess for Christmas’.

Michael Damian directed and co-wrote with his wife a romantic comedy ‘The Sweeter Side of Life’ in 2012 and the film premiered on the Hallmark Channel on January 19, 2012.

His theatre works include ‘Venture Country Youth Experimental Theatre’ in 1971/1973 and ‘Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat’ as Joseph in 1993/1994.

Awards, Nominations

Michael Damian was awarded Boston International Film Festival Award in the category Best Narrative Feature for ‘Hot Tamale in 2006, the Young Artists Award in the category Best Young Actor in Daytime Soap for ‘The Young and the Restless in 1983, and 1984, LA Femme International Filmmaker Award in the category Best Writer for “Moondance Alexander’ in 2007 and has been nominated several times during his career.
