Early Life & Biography

Key was the son of black father Leroy McDuffie and white mother, Carrie Herr. He was adopted at an early age by Michael Key and Patricia Walsh, who were both social workers in Detroit. His adoptive parents were also a black man and a white woman like his birth parents. Because of his biological father, Key had two half brothers. One of whom was comic book writer Dwayne McDuffie. But, his siblings died at a very early age.

Key attended the University of Detroit Mercy, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in theater in 1993. Then he earned a Master of Fine Arts in theater at Pennsylvania State University in 1996. He was a brother of Phi Kappa Theta, while he was studying at the University of Detroit Mercy.
