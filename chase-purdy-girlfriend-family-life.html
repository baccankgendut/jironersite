Chase Purdy Wiki: Parents & Family Life

Born as Chase R. Purdy on 11 November 1999 in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, he is 21 years of age. His parents Dr. Charlie Purdy and Rebecca Purdy raised him in Meridian, Mississippi.


His father Charlie is a family medicine doctor in Meridian, Mississippi. He is affiliated with a number of hospitals, including Anderson Regional Medical Center.

Chase has five siblings in the family. He has two brothers Zachary Purdy and Andrew Purdy, and three younger sisters, including Ellie Purdy.


The young racer resides with his mother and other family members in North Carolina. Meanwhile, his father resides in Meridian. Moreover, his siblings reside in Meridian and Tuscaloosa.

Purdy attended Lamar School in his hometown and later went to Cox Mill High School in Concord, North Carolina. Currently, he is pursuing his further education at the University of Mississippi.

His Career Achievements & Net Worth

Chase used to race in go-karts and won Talladega Superspeedway at an early age. Afterward, he moved to Bandolero cars, Legend cars, Late Model Stock cars, and super late model cars.

In 2017, he made his debut in the NASCAR K&N Pro Series East, finishing fourth in the season. Later, he started driving full-time in the ARCA Racing Series in 2018.

Purdy made his NASCAR Camping World Truck Series debut at Martinsville Speedway on 17 October 2018. Likewise, he competed part-time in the NASCAR Camping World Truck Series in 2020. Recently, he became a full-time driver for GMS in the Truck Series in 2021.
