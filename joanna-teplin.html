The Home Edit Joanna Teplin Wiki | Bio

43 years old Joanna Teplin (born in 1979) is an American home decor expert, TV personality, and businesswoman who created a buzz in Hollywood with the second season of Netflix’s beloved reality show “The Home Edit.”

It is a show which Joanna and her business partner Clea Shearer started in 2020 where they show their skills of managing/arranging stuff in celebrities’ houses. The first season of this show was able to win the hearts of many viewers, which is why they were waiting for the release of the second season.

How Did Joanna Teplin Become a Decor expert?

In 2013 Joanna and her husband came to Nashville because Jeremy got assigned there by his company. 2 years later, she contacted Clea, and they found many things similar in their nature. After that, they started their own company named ” The Home Edit,” which specialized in improving the aesthetics of the home interior.

It took a lot of hard work to grow the company, but they managed it, and now even many celebrities contact Teplin if they ever need her assistance.

When the business for Joanna’s company was at its peak, she thought about using her filmmaking skills and discussed it with Clea Shearer. Their discussion concluded with making the show, and later they got approval from Netflix.

There are three books that Teplin and Shearer launched over time, namely, The Home Edit Workbook, The Home Edit Life, and The Home Edit.

Childhood, Family, and Education

In 1979, Joanna Teplin (age 43 years) took birth in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, United States, at the house of her Christian parents. Like her mother, Teplin also loved creating beautiful things, painting, and filmmaking.

Passion and love for art motivated Joanna to pursue BA in filmmaking from Santa Barbara’s The University of California. Her first job was at Union Street Papery, a famous spot for creative stationery in San Francisco.

Who is the Husband of Joanna Teplin?

Teplin is married to a New Jersey man named Jeremy Rubin, who works as a manager of the promotions department at the famous recording company Columbia Records. The exact date of the marriage of this cute couple is not known, but it has been more than half a decade since they tied the knot.
