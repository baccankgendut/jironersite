Davante Adams, a wide receiver for the National Football League’s Las Vegas Raiders, was born on December 24, 1992.

He attended Fresno State during his collegiate football career, and the Green Bay Packers selected him in the second round of the 2014 NFL Draft.

The Las Vegas Raiders wide receiver currently lives in a $12 million mansion in The Ridges, Las Vegas with his wife and child.
