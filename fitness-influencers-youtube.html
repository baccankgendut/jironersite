1. Yoga With Adriene

You might have heard about Yoga with Adriene if you are fitness conscious. She provides high-quality yoga videos for people to help them achieve a healthy mind and body.

She provides varieties of yoga so people can choose and practice according to their desire and needs. Her Yoga is also directed to beginners and provides foundations of Yoga.

She also has weight-reducing and sweat-inducing Yoga videos on the channel. Due to the wide range of Yoga videos, people have liked her video, and there are thousands to millions of views on her each uploaded video.

Adriene gets hundred thousand to million views on each video uploaded on her Youtube Channel. Her channel has about 11.5 million subscribers, with a total view of 1,239,475,196. She shares two videos in a month on her YouTube channel.

2. Kayla Itsines

Kayla Itsines has been working as a fitness instructor for women since 2008. Her channel has 413k subscribers and a total of 5,499,560 views.

Kayla is known for her Bikini Body Guide workouts. She has a mission to bring incredible confidence and pride to women worldwide.

She is providing the how-to to people who want to change their life. Her workouts can be found in the SWEAT app too. Her workouts are in the five programs High Intensity, High-Intensity Strength, Low Impact, Zero Equipment, and Post Pregnancy.

She also has achieved the top honors on Forbes' list of Top Fitness Influencers and Time's top 30 most influential people on the internet due to her experience as a dynamic fitness influencer. Her workouts have been loved by over 50,000,000 women worldwide on Sweat.

3. Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a fitness YouTube channel run by the couples Daniel and Kelli. The channel has 6.66 million subscribers and a total of 1,258,940,949 views.

The channel was created in 2010, and since then, they have provided more than 600 free, full-length workout videos.

Daniel and Kelli believe that smart workouts and healthy whole foods can improve people's health and quality of life. They share not only workouts but also health information on their YouTube channel. So, people can find a wide range of content and choose one they are more intrigued to know about.

The channel is focused on providing unbiased workout, nutrition, and health information.

4. POPSUGAR Fitness

POPSUGAR Fitness is a Fitness channel that provides fresh fitness tutorials and exercises ranging from strengthening, heart-pumping, and empowering to downright feel-good workouts.

The provided workouts can be easily done at home. The fitness channel provides from full-body routines to moves focused on arms, glutes, or core. Similarly, the workouts can be done from 10 minutes to 30 minutes.

Their workouts are targeted from beginner to advanced in bodyweight cardio, dance, Tabata, Pilates, barre, and many more.

Class Fitsugar and Dance Fitsugar, provided by the channel, allow people to do workouts and swear alongside fitness experts and trainers.

The channel provides workouts from 10 to 15 minutes to 30 minutes, and people can choose the amount of time they want to exercise and choose videos accordingly. They also provide new videos by premiering them on interval days in a week.
