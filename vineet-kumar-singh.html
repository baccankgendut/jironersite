Education

Vineet Kumar Singh attended RA Podar Medical College and graduated with a Bachelor of Ayurveda, Medicine, and Surgery (B.A.M.S.) degree. Then, he received his MD (Doctor of Medicine) in Ayurveda from the Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur.

    Vineet Kumar Singh Facts

  • Growing up, he took interest in sports and played basketball on a national level.
  • His acting pursuits started when he came to Mumbai to participate in Superstars Talent Hunt, which he subsequently won. Actor-director Mahesh Manjrekar was one of the judges and he offered Vineet a role in the 2002 Sanjay Dutt-starrer action drama film Pitaah.
  • He also worked as an associate/assistant director for Mahesh Manjrekar in films such as Viruddh and Deh.
  • It was in 2007 that Vineet Kumar Singh decided to solely focus on his acting career so he left direction.
  • He played Danish Khan in the critically-acclaimed 2012 Hindi-language crime action film Gangs of Wasseypur – Part 1. He co-starred in the project alongside Manoj Bajpayee, Richa Chadda, Reema Sen, Piyush Mishra, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Pankaj Tripathi, Huma Qureshi, Anurita Jha, and Tigmanshu Dhulia.
  • Vineet reprised his role of Danish Khan in Gangs of Wasseypur – Part 2, the 2nd installment of the Gangs of Wasseypur series.
  • Vineet Kumar Singh co-starred as an aspiring boxer named Shravan Kumar Singh in the 2017 Hindi-language sports drama film Mukkabaaz. For this role, he won the 2018 FOI Online Award for “Best Actor in a Leading Role” and the 2019 Critics’ Choice Film Award for “Best Actor” as well as was nominated for a Filmfare Award and a Screen Award in the “Best Actor (Critics)” category.
  • He brought to life the character of agent Veer Singh in the Netflix spy thriller series Bard of Blood in 2019.
  • In 2019, he played Pharsua in the Hindi-language dramedy film Aadhaar.
  • Vineet was cast as Fight commander officer Dileep Singh in the 2020 Hindi-language biographical drama film Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl, starring Janhvi Kapoor, Pankaj Tripathi, and Angad Bedi.
  • In 2020, he appeared as Commandant Vikram Sirohi in the zombie horror streaming television series Betaal.
  • On June 20, 2023, Vineet’s mother passed away at the age of 72.
