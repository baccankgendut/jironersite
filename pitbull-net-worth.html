Who Is Pitbull?

Pitbull is a famous rapper and singer who has been making hit songs for over two decades.

Pitbull’s real name is Armando Christian Pérez, but he chose his stage name because of the dogs’ tenacity and strength.

Pitbull started his career in 2001, when he was signed by Luther Campbell’s Luke Records.

He then worked with Lil Jon on his 2002 album Kings of Crunk, which gave him his first mainstream exposure.

Pitbull released his debut album M.I.A.M.I. (Money Is A Major Issue) in 2004, which featured the hit single “Culo”.

Pitbull later released several more albums, such as El Mariel (2006), The Boatlift (2007), Rebelution (2009), Planet Pit (2011), Global Warming (2012), Globalization (2014), Dale (2015), Climate Change (2017), and Libertad 548 (2019).

Some of his most popular songs include “I Know You Want Me (Calle Ocho)”, “Hotel Room Service”, “Give Me Everything”, “Rain Over Me”, “International Love”, “Feel This Moment”, “Timber”, “Fireball”, “Time of Our Lives”, and “Don’t Stop the Party”.

Pitbull has also collaborated with many artists from different genres and backgrounds, such as Jennifer Lopez, Enrique Iglesias, Shakira, Christina Aguilera, Usher, Kesha, Ne-Yo, Marc Anthony, Sofia Vergara, and more.

He has also performed at various events and shows, such as the FIFA World Cup, the Super Bowl, the Grammys, the Latin Grammys, the American Music Awards, and more.

Pitbull has also ventured into other fields, such as acting, hosting, producing, and philanthropy.

He has appeared in movies such as Epic, The Angry Birds Movie 2, and UglyDolls, TV shows such as Empire and Shark Tank, and web series such as Pitbull: Beyond Worldwide.

He has also hosted events such as the American Music Awards, the Latin American Music Awards, and New Year’s Revolution.

Pitbull Wife

Pitbull does not have a wife at the moment, but he was briefly married to Lorraine Nash in 2019.

Lorraine Nash is the mother of Pitbull’s best friend and fellow YouTuber Jason Nash.

Pitbull married Lorraine as a prank on Jason, who joked that Pitbull would never get married.

Pitbull flew to Boston, proposed to Lorraine, and took her to Las Vegas for a quick wedding.

They also went to Hawaii for a honeymoon.

Pitbull announced his marriage on social media, shocking his fans and friends.

However, the marriage was not meant to last, and Pitbull filed for divorce after one month.

He said he was heartbroken and wished Lorraine nothing but the best.

The divorce was finalized in November 2019, ending their short-lived marriage.

He has also made money from his own label, merchandise, endorsements, and other ventures.
