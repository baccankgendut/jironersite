Jackie Gleason Biography Timeline

Jackie Gleason was an American actor, comedian, writer, composer and conductor. He was born on February 26, 1916, in Brooklyn, New York, and passed away on June 24, 1987, in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. He is best known for his role as Ralph Kramden in the TV series “The Honeymooners.” Throughout his career, Gleason appeared in numerous films, TV shows and stage productions and was regarded as one of the greatest comedians of his time.
